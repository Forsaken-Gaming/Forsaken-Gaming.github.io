Special Delivery: VR

"Special Delivery: VR" is a fan-made adaptation of Illumix's "Five Nights at Freddy's AR: Special Delivery" recreated within Unreal Engine.
The game will be able to be played in either VR or Flat Mode.

My Work:

  • Faithfully recreated the original game's "Encounter" systen.
  • Recreated the original game's character shaders within Unreal Engine.
  • Faithfully remade the original game's audio design within "Encounters"
