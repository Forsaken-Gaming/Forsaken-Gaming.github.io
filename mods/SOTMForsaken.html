Secret of the Mimic: Forsaken over Arnold

This mod for Five Nights at Freddy's Secret of the Mimic replaces Arnold's arms with my character's arms.
When a new game comes out, whether it be FNaF or something else, I enjoy seeing if I can mod my character over a progonist. It's a fun habit I've started.
So when Secret of the Mimic came out, I did just that. I've also taken it a step farther and made a custom item you can pick up and deposit in the inventory system.
I even gave him custom audio of myself shouting when he hits the floor. That required modifying the game's Wwise Audio implementation.
