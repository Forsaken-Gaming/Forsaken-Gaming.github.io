THE JOY OF CREATION Demo: Office Hard Mode

This mod for THE JOY OF CREATION (TJOC) makes the night much more difficult to complete.
I took inspiration from a cut ideas that the developer Nikson had shared. One being that Chica's cupcakes were going to be able to appear in the hallways.
After decompiling all of the animatronics and the cupcakes, I was able to add that back myself. I was then able to tweak the AI of the characters to do whatever I wanted with.
This mod was a bit tricky to get going because this game is developed with Unreal Engine 5. This version of Unreal is a bit more annoying to mod, but I figured out the differences and I was off!
I had made two versions of the mod. One was an initial which featured more changes and was double the length. After listening to feedback, I toned it down a lot and made it more beat-able.

Features:

  • All Animatronics start right away at 12 AM.
  • Inner Freddy will kill you after 6 AM if you flash the incorrect side of a hallway.
  • All idle timers lowered except for Freddy. Animatronics will come back faster.
  • Foxy's required hits on cameras are doubled, but the time it takes to hit him (stare at him) is lowered.
  • There are two new cupcake spawn points, one in the closet and one in the hallway on the right. There are none outside.
