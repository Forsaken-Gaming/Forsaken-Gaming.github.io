Security Breach: Glamrock Freddy's Blue Eyes Restored

This mod for Five Nights at Freddy's Security Breach makes it so Glamrock Freddy has blue eyes before upgrading him with Roxy's eyes.
After the upgrade, his eyes will be yellow like normal since Roxy's eyes are yellow.
Freddy's eyes also swap when you plug the replacement ones in during the upgrade procedure.
This required making a new eye material based off of Freddy's existing one. I wanted either one to be used rather than outright replacing his old one.
I then used UAssetGUI to change the materials the game looks for to find Freddy's eye materials.
To my surprise, the Upgrade and Test minigame was set up to look for a replacement eye material already. So changing the reference of the base material to my new one did the trick!
